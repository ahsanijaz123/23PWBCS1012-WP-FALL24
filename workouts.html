WORKOUT ROUTINES

Plan 1: Beginner's Fitness (30 minutes, 3 times a week)

1. Warm-up (5 minutes)

- Jumping jacks: 30 seconds

- Dynamic stretching: leg swings, arm circles, hip circles

2. Strength Training (20 minutes)

- Squats: 3 sets, 10 reps

- Push-ups: 3 sets, 10 reps

- Lunges: 3 sets, 10 reps (per leg)

- Planks: 3 sets, 30-second hold

3. Cardio (5 minutes)

- Brisk walking or jogging in place

4. Cool-down (5 minutes)

- Static stretching: hamstrings, quadriceps, chest, back

Plan 2: Intermediate Strength Training (45 minutes, 4 times a week)

: Chest and Triceps

1. Warm-up (5 minutes)

2. Barbell Bench Press: 3 sets, 8-12 reps

3. Incline Dumbbell Press: 3 sets, 10-15 reps

4. Tricep Pushdowns: 3 sets, 12-15 reps

5. Tricep Dips: 3 sets, 12-15 reps

6. Cool-down (5 minutes)

Back and Biceps

>1. Warm-up (5 minutes)

2. Pull-ups: 3 sets, 8-12 reps

3. Barbell Rows: 3 sets, 8-12 reps

4. Dumbbell Bicep Curls: 3 sets, 10-15 reps

5. Hammer Curls: 3 sets, 10-15 reps

6. Cool-down (5 minutes)
